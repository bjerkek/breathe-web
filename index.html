Let's take a moment and breathe

We all need to pause every now and then.

Taking a few minutes during the day and just let go of everything is good for the mind.

Breathing is easy

When you're ready, start with a few deep breaths and then gently close your eyes.

Try to breathe naturally. Rather than letting the mind leading the breath, let the breath leading the mind.

Notice the sensation of the breath, where you feel it in the body and try to follow that raising and falling sensation.

If you like you can count the breaths to help you maintain the focus. Counting 1 on the inhale, 2 on the exhale, all the way up to ten before you start over.
